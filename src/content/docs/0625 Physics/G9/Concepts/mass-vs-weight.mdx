## 质量与重量的区别

| 方面                  | Mass                           | Weight                                                     |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| 量的类型                | Scalar quantity (no direction) | Vector quantity (direction acts towards center of gravity) |
| 随位置的变化            | Constant everywhere            | Varies with gravitational field strength (g)               |
| 测量工具                | Digital or beam balance        | Spring balance or force meter                              |
| SI Unit                 | kg                             | Newton (N)                                                 |
| 表示                    | Amount of matter in an object  | Gravitational pull on an object                            |
| 示例                    | 50 kg on Earth and Moon        | 490 N on Earth, 80 N on Moon                               |

**Scalar**：只有大小（量级）和单位。  
**Vector**：既有量级又有方向。
