## Measurement of Length

- Measured using a **metre rule**
- SI Unit of length is the **metre (m)**

### Prefixes of Metre

- $ 1 $ m → $ 100 $ cm
- $ 1 $ km → $ 1000 $ m

### Class Activity 1: Measure thickness of one slotted mass

**a) Process**

1. Take 3 slotted masses and stack them together.
2. Measure the combined height (e.g., 3 cm).
3. Thickness of one mass = Total height ÷ number of masses (e.g., $ 3 \div 3 = 1 $ cm).

**b) Sources of errors / uncertainty**

- **Parallax error**: Reading the value when eyes are not perpendicular to the scale.
- Not measuring from the zero line of the metre rule.

**How to minimise error**

- Position eye level perpendicular to the scale (non-parallax method).
- Place the metre rule directly against the object.

### Thickness / External Diameter of a Tube

**Process**

1. Put boiling tube between two wooden blocks.
2. Measure the distance of separation with the ruler.

**Precautions**

1. Avoid measuring at the lips of the boiling tube.
