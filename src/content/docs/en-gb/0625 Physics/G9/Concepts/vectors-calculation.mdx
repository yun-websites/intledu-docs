## Vectors

When two perpendicular vectors act on an object (horizontal and vertical), the resultant is obtained by completing a right-angled triangle.

The magnitude of the resultant is found using:
$$ R = \sqrt{A^2 + B^2} $$

In a scale drawing, choose an appropriate scale and draw both vectors at right angles; the diagonal represents the resultant.

### Resultant Vectors – Triangle Method

$$\vec{AC} = \vec{AB} + \vec{BC} \quad \text{(Vector addition)}$$

$$R^2 = A^2 + B^2$$
