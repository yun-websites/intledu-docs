## The Critical Angle

Snell's law:

$$
n_1\text{ }\sin\text{ }\theta_1 = n_2\text{ }\sin\text{ }\theta_2
$$

At critical angle:

$$
n_1\text{ }\sin\text{ }C = n_2 \sin 90^\circ
$$

$$
n\text{ }\sin\text{ }C = 1
$$

$$
\sin\text{ }C = \frac{1}{n}
$$

$$
C = \sin^{-1}\left(\frac{1}{n}\right)
$$

Refractive index is inversely proportional to critical angle.
