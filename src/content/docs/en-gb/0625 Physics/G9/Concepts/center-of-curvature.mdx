The center of curvature is the center of the sphere of which the lens is part. It is denoted as 2F or C.

Key points along the principal axis are:
- Optical centre (O): the centre of the lens.
- Focal point (F): located at a distance equal to the focal length from O.
- Centre of curvature (C or 2F): located at twice the focal length from O.

The value \( c \) (centre of curvature) is used to construct the lens as the centre of circles.
