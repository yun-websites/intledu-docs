## Key Equations and Formulae

| Physical Quantity | Formula                     | Units          |
| ----------------- | --------------------------- | -------------- |
| Weight            | $ W = m \times g $          | Newton (N)     |
| Density           | $ \rho = \frac{m}{V} $      | kg/m³ or g/cm³ |
| Volume of Cube    | $ V = L \times W \times H $ | m³ or cm³      |
| Volume of Sphere  | $ V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3 $ | m³ or cm³      |
