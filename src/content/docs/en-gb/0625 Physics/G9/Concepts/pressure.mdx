## Pressure

Pressure is the amount of force pushing on an area of a surface.  
Pressure is caused by any contact force pushing on an object.  
Pressure is force acting normal per unit area.

### In solids

$$ P = \frac{F}{A} = \frac{mg}{A} $$

$$ P_{max} = \frac{F}{A_{min}} $$

$$ P_{min} = \frac{F}{A_{max}} $$"

### In liquids

$$ P = h \rho g $$

### In U-tube

Given the $$ P_{\text{atm}} = 76 \, \text{cm Hg} $$. Calculate the $$ P_g $$ in

a) $$ P = 96 \, \text{cm Hg} $$

b) $$ P = 130560 \, \text{Pa} $$ (as density of Hg is $$ 13600 \, \text{kg/m}^3 $$)

a) $$ P_g' = P_{\text{atm}} + \Delta h = 76 + 20 = 96 \, \text{cm Hg} $$

b) $$ P_g = P_g' \times 13600 \times 10 = 130560 \, \text{Pa} $$
