## Hooke's Law

Provided the limit of proportionality is not exceeded:  
Directly proportional means the graph passes through the origin and is a straight line.

The limit of proportionality is the point where the material stops obeying Hooke's law.

$$ F = k \Delta x $$

- $ F $ is the applied force (load)
- $ \Delta x $ is the extension
- $ k $ is the spring constant

### Sources of Error

- Rule or spring is not placed vertically.
- The meter rule should be close to the spring (to reduce parallax error).
