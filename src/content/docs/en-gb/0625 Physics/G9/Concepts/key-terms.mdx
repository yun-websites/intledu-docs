## Key Terms

| Keyword     | Definition                                              | Unit / Symbol  |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | -------------- |
| Mass (m)    | The amount of matter in a body. Constant everywhere.    | Kilogram (kg)  |
| Weight (W)  | Force of gravity acting on a mass. Depends on location. | Newton (N)     |
| Density (ρ) | Mass per unit volume of a substance.                    | kg/m³ or g/cm³ |
| Volume (V)  | Space occupied by an object or substance.               | m³ or cm³      |
| Buoyancy    | Upward force exerted by a fluid on a submerged object.  | Newton (N)     |
