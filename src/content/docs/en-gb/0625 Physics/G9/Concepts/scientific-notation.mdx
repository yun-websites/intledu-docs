## Scientific Notation / Standard Form

$$
M \times 10^n
$$

- $ M $ is the coefficient ($ 1 \leq |M| < 10 $)
- 10 is the base
- $ n $ is the exponent or power of 10

**Examples**

1. $ 500.03 $ cm = $ 500.03 $ m = $ 5.0003 \times 10^2 $ m (usually $ 5.0 \times 10^2 $)
2. $ 3.68 $ g = $ 3.68 \times 10^-3 $ kg
