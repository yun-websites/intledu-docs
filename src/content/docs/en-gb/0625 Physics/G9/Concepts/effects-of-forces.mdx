## Effects of Forces

Force can:

- Change the state of motion.
- Change shape.
- Cause a turning effect.
- Speed up or slow down motion.
- Stop motion.

### Summary of Effects

Force can cause:

1. Change of shape.
2. Change of state of motion:
    - Speed up.
    - Slow down.
    - Stop.
3. Change direction.

Measurement of force is done using a force meter (Newton meter / spring balance).
