## Conservation of Momentum

Momentum is conserved in a closed system (no external forces).  
Total momentum before collision equals total momentum after collision.

### High-Risk Misconceptions

- Moment ≠ Momentum
- Impulse ≠ Force
- Airbags reduce force by increasing impact time (stopping time)
- Momentum is conserved in closed systems
- Force depends on stopping time

### Language Traps

- Avoid mixing the words "moment" and "momentum".
- Use "change in momentum" in explanations involving impulse.
- Do not use "impact force" without explaining what happens to impact time.
