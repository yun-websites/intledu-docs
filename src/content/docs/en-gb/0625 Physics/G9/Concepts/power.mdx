## Power

**Definition:**  
Power is the rate of doing work or the rate of energy transfer.

**Key idea:**  
Same work done in less time → greater power

**Equation:**  
$$ P = \frac{E}{t} $$ or $$ P = \frac{W}{t} $$

**Where:**

- $$ P $$ = power (W)
- $$ E $$ = energy transferred (J) or $$ W $$ = work done (J)
- $$ t $$ = time taken (s)

**Exam tips:**

- mention time when explaining power
- Use ‘rate of energy transfer’
- Do not confuse power with energy
