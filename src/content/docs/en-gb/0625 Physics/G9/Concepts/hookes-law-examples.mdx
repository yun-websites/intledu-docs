## Example Questions

**Example 1**

$$
F = k \Delta x \implies \frac{F}{\Delta x} = k
$$

$$
\frac{F}{2-0} = k
$$

**Example 2**

$$
F = k \Delta x
$$

$$
2 = 70 \times \Delta x
$$

$$
\Delta x = \frac{2}{70} = 0.1
$$
