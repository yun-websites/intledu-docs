## Impulse

Impulse is derived from Newton's second law:

$$ F = m a = m \left( \frac{v - u}{t} \right) = \frac{mv - mu}{t} $$

Thus:

$$ Ft = mv - mu $$

Impulse equals the change in momentum.  
Impulse is the product of force and time.  
Unit: Ns (Newton second).
