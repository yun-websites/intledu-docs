## Efficiency

**Definition:**  
Efficiency describes how much of the input energy or power is transferred usefully.

**Energy form:**  
$$ \text{Efficiency} = \frac{\text{useful energy output}}{\text{total energy input}} $$

**Power form:**  
$$ \text{Efficiency} = \frac{\text{useful power output}}{\text{total power input}} $$

**Percentage efficiency:**  
$$ (\%) \, \text{efficiency} = \text{efficiency} \times 100\% $$

**Key ideas:**

- No device is 100% efficient
- Energy is conserved
- Wasted energy is transferred to the surroundings
