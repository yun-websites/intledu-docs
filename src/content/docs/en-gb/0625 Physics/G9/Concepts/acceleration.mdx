## Acceleration

**Definition**: Acceleration is the rate of change in speed.

$$ a = \frac{V*{\text{final}} - V*{\text{start}}}{t} $$

- $ a $: acceleration (m/s²)
- Change in speed (m/s) divided by time (s)
- It is also the gradient of a speed–time graph.
