## Graph of Force vs Extension

The graph is a straight line passing through the origin.  
Force (N) is plotted on the vertical axis and extension on the horizontal axis.  
The gradient of the line equals the spring constant $k$.

The point labelled $P$ is the **limit of proportionality** — beyond this point the spring stops obeying Hooke's law.
