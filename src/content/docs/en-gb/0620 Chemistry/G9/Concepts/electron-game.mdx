**A more "physical" summary (this is high-scoring thinking)**

The entire chapter is actually about one thing:

> The "tug-of-war" between atoms and electrons

- Metals: Release electrons
- Non-metals: Capture electrons
- Trends: Who can control electrons more easily

If you can reduce all phenomena to this statement, the periodic table is no longer rote memorization, but an "electron power map".
