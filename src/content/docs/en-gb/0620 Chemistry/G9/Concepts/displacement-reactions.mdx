**Core philosophy: More reactive → Takes position**

## Halogen Displacement

Reactivity:
Cl > Br > I

$$
Cl_2 + 2KBr \rightarrow 2KCl + Br_2
$$

Essence:
**Stronger oxidizer displaces weaker one**

## Metal Displacement

Reactivity:
Zn > Fe > Cu

$$
Zn + FeCl_2 \rightarrow ZnCl_2 + Fe
$$

Essence:
**Metal that loses electrons more easily → Reduces others**
