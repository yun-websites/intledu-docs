## Core Concept

- The maximum amount of solid that can dissolve at a certain temperature

👉 Essence:
**Dissolution = The limit of particles being "pulled away"**

## Types of Solutions

- Unsaturated → Can still dissolve more
- Saturated → Reached the limit
- Supersaturated → Overloaded (unstable)

👉 Supersaturated is interesting:
Like an "over-compressed system" that could collapse (crystallize) at any moment

## Special Cases

- Suspension → Insoluble, will settle eventually
- Precipitation → Reaction produces insoluble substance

👉 Essence:
**Not all particles want to mix together**
