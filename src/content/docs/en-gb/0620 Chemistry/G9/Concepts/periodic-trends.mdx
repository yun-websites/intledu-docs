## Across a Period

**Essence: Increasing nuclear charge → Enhanced electron attraction**

- Metallic → Non-metallic

(Deeper level: Atoms become "more eager for electrons")

## Down a Group

**Key mechanism: Increasing electron shells → Enhanced shielding effect**

The inner electrons "block" the nuclear charge, weakening the attraction felt by outer electrons.

### Reactivity

- Metals → Increase (easier to lose electrons)
- Non-metals → Decrease (harder to gain electrons)
