The atom has a dense core at the center called the nucleus, surrounded by shells (energy levels).

## Properties of Subatomic Particles

| Property | Protons | Neutrons | Electrons |
|----------|---------|----------|-----------|
| Charge   | Positive | Neutral | Negative |
| Mass     | 1       | 1       | 1/1870    |
| Location | Inside nucleus | Nucleus | Outside the nucleus |

## Neutral Atom

Number of protons = number of electrons → electrically neutral.

## Structure of an Atom (Helium example)

| Particle | Mass | Charge |
|----------|------|--------|
| Proton   | 2    | +2     |
| Neutron  | 2    | 0      |
| Electron | 0    | -2     |
| **Helium atom** | **total 4** | **total 0** |
