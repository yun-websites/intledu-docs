## Physical Trends

- Melting / boiling point ↓

## Reaction with Water

**Essence: Lose electrons → Form alkali + Hydrogen**

$$
M + H_2O \rightarrow H_2 + MOH
$$

(More strictly should be balanced, but IGCSE often writes it this way)
