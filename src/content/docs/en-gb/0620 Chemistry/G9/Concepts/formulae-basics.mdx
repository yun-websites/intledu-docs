## Element
A substance that consists of one type of atom.  
In reality they consist of atoms with the same number of protons.  
The smallest particle of an element that can take part in a reaction.

## Molecule
Two or more atoms bonded together.

## Compound
A molecule that contains different atoms.

## Ion
A species that contains one or more types of atoms that has an overall charge (negative/positive).

Atoms bond to form molecules, which can be elements or compounds depending on whether the atoms are the same or different.
