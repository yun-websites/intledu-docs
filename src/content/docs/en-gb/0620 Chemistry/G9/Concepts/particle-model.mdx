**Core: Matter properties = Particle arrangement + Particle motion + Forces**

This is the "master switch" for the entire chapter - all subsequent phenomena can be derived from this model.

## States of Matter

### Solid

**Mechanism: Strong forces + Fixed positions**

- Closely packed
- Regular lattice arrangement
- Vibrate in fixed positions
- High density
- Not compressible
- Does not flow

👉 Essence in one sentence:
**Particles are "locked", can only vibrate, not move around**

### Liquid

**Mechanism: Weaker forces + Slidable**

- Closely packed (but irregular)
- Move past each other
- Fixed volume
- Not fixed shape
- Flows

👉 Essence in one sentence:
**Particles start to "break free", can move around each other**

### Gas

**Mechanism: Almost no forces + Free motion**

- Far apart
- Random motion
- No fixed shape or volume
- Easily compressed
- Low density

👉 Essence in one sentence:
**Particles are completely free, like a group of caffeinated electrons**

### Gas Behaviour

- Temperature ↑ → particles move faster → volume ↑
- Pressure ↓ → particles spread out → volume ↑

👉 Core is not formulas, but:
**The game between space and kinetic energy**
