## Electrons in Shells

The colors produced in flame tests are due to electrons in the atom moving between two different electron shells.

- Increasing energy and distance from nucleus  
- First level: only room for two electrons  
- Second level: only room for eight electrons  
- Third level: sometimes appears full with eight electrons but can expand to a total of 18

## Examples

**Lithium** ($ _3\mathrm{Li} $): (2, 1)  
**Sodium** ($ _{11}\mathrm{Na} $): (2, 8, 1)  
**Potassium** ($ _{19}\mathrm{K} $): (2, 8, 8, 1)
