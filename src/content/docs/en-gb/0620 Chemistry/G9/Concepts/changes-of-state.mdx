## Energy Perspective

This is the key unifying logic:

- **Endothermic** → Particles gain energy → More free
- **Exothermic** → Particles lose energy → More restricted

## Phase Transitions

### Endothermic

- Melting: solid → liquid
- Boiling: liquid → gas
- Evaporation: liquid → gas (surface only)
- Sublimation: solid → gas

### Exothermic

- Condensation: gas → liquid
- Freezing: liquid → solid

## Boiling vs Evaporation

- Boiling → Occurs throughout (bulk)
- Evaporation → Only at the surface

👉 Deep difference:
**Whether all particles have enough energy to escape**
