## Charge of Common Ions

| Charge |                         Ions                          |
| :----: | :---------------------------------------------------- |
|   1+   |              $Ag^+$ and all Group I ions              |
|   2+   | $Zn^{2+}$, $Cu^{2+}$, $Fe^{2+}$ and all Group II ions |
|   3+   |           $Fe^{3+}$ and all Group III ions            |
|   2-   |    $SO_4^{2-}$, $CO_3^{2-}$ and all Group VI ions     |
|   1-   |  $NO_3^-$, $OH^-$, $HCO_3^-$ and all Group VII ions   |

## Determining Formulae

Overall charge of compound = 0.  
Total positive charge = total negative charge.  
If more than one of a complex ion is needed, enclose in brackets ().
