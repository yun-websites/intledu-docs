## Core Mechanism

- Random motion of particles
- Move from high → low concentration

👉 Note:
This is not "conscious flow", but a **statistical result** (random → macroscopically directional)

## Gas vs Liquid

- Gas → faster
- Liquid → slower

👉 Reason:
**Particle freedom + Kinetic energy difference**

## Factors Affecting Diffusion

- Temperature ↑ → kinetic energy ↑ → faster
- Molecular mass ↑ → movement ↓ → slower

👉 Deep logic:

> Diffusion speed ≈ "ability to move randomly"
