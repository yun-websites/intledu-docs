- $Na$ — 1 atom of sodium
- $2Na$ — 2 atoms of sodium
- $N$ — 1 atom of nitrogen
- $2N$ — 2 separate atoms of nitrogen
- $N_2$ — 1 molecule of nitrogen (contains 2 atoms of nitrogen joined together)
- $2N_2$ — 2 molecules of nitrogen (each contains 2 atoms of nitrogen joined together)
- $H_2O$ — 1 molecule of water (contains 2 hydrogen atoms joined to 1 atom of oxygen)
