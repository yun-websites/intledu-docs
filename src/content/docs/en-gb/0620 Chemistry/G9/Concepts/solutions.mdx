## Basic Definitions

- Solute → Being dissolved
- Solvent → Dissolves others
- Solution → Homogeneous mixture

👉 Essence:
**Particle-level "mixing in"**

## Mass vs Volume

$$
M_{solution} = M_{solute} + M_{solvent}
$$

$$
V_{solution} \ne V_{solute} + V_{solvent}
$$

👉 This is counterintuitive but scientific:

- Mass is conserved (particles don't disappear)
- Volume is not conserved (particles can "fit into gaps")

Like pouring sand into rock crevices - space utilization changes.
