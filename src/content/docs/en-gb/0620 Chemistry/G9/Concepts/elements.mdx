**Definition of Element**:  
A substance that cannot be further divided into simpler substances by chemical methods. (All the atoms of an element contain the same number of protons.)

## Examples

| Atom      | Format                     | Proton number | Neutron number | Electron number | Mass number |
| --------- | -------------------------- | ------------- | -------------- | --------------- | ----------- |
| hydrogen  | $ ^{1}_{1}\mathrm{H} $    | 1             | 0              | 1               | 1           |
| chlorine  | $ ^{35}_{17}\mathrm{Cl} $ | 17            | 18             | 17              | 35          |
| cobalt    | $ ^{59}_{27}\mathrm{Co} $ | 27            | 32             | 27              | 59          |
| iodine    | $ ^{127}_{53}\mathrm{I} $ | 53            | 74             | 53              | 127         |
| germanium | $ ^{74}_{32}\mathrm{Ge} $ | 32            | 42             | 32              | 74          |
